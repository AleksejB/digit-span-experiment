<h1 style="text-align: center;">Digit Span Experiment</h1>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><strong>Instructions</strong>: Upon pressing the START button, you will be shown a series of random numbers from 0-9 inclusevly. Each number will be shown one at a time with a small time period to remember the number, after the x amount of numbers were shown, there will be small delay and you will be prompted to write down the numbers shown. You must write the numbers in order they were shown with no spaces in between. To submit you answer press NEXT, after which the process will restart. The amount of numbers shown will increase if your previous answer was correct. The starting amount of numbers is 3, if you answer correctly, next trial will show 4 numbers and so on until 10. If you answer incorrectly the current attempt will be over and you will automatically start a new attempt. You must complete all 5 attempts.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Thank you for participating, press START to begin.</p>
